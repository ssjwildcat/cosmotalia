Ebonik


The Sentinel of Darkness, The Realist

Element: Darkness
Color: Black
Patron to: Lawyers, Witches, Scientists, Magicians
Associated Season: Middle Autumn
Personality: Logical, Critical, Distant
Common Pronouns: she/her, they/them
Sacred Land: The Inkine Rocks
Other Information:
  • Her most popular depiction blends elements from jackals and moths. She is often depicted with a halo of darkness and casting shadows as she walks.
  • Her titles comes from her realist nature and no-nonsense ways. This makes her a rival to Pirism, who is characterized as more of a dreamer.
  • “Ebonik” comes from a corruption on “ebony”.
