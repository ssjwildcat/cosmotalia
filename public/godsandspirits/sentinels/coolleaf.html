Coolleaf


The Sentinel of Earth, The Nomad

Element: Earth
Color: Pale Green
Patron to: Wanderers, Explorers, Miners, Guests
Associated Season: Early Autumn
Personality: Carefree, Cheerful, Sociable
Common Pronouns: they/them, he/him
Sacred Land: The Coolleaf Forest
Other Information:
  • Many depictions depict him with their mane in various stage of growth; with berries, flowers, or dead in winter. The berries from his mane are said to reinvigorate.
  • His most popular depiction blends elements from plants, deer and gargoyles. He is depicted with a halo made of greenery.
  • It’s said he was the most traveled of the Sentinels and encouraged curiosity in his children.
  • The Coolleaf Forest was named after them. Coolleaf itself is a simple portmanteau of “cool” and “leaf”.
