Jaquara


The Sentinel of Nature, The Caretender

Element: Nature
Color: Light Green
Patron to: Mothers and Children, Nurses, Herbalists, Transgender Creatures
Associated Season: Late Spring
Personality: Sweet, Patient, Fiercely Protective
Common Pronouns: she/her
Sacred Land: Jaquara Rainforest
Other Information:
  • Her most popular depiction blends elements from raptors, jaguars and kirin.
  • Her title comes from her protective nature over not just nature but all creatures.
  • In recent years, she and Sapphidel have been claimed to be patron to transgender creatures, due to their connection to the healing elements (which are often used in transitions).
  • The Jaquara Rainforest is named for her. “Jaquara” itself is a corruption of “jaguar”.
