Pirism


The Sentinel of Light, The Dreamer

Element: Light
Color: White
Patron to: Thieves, Tricksters, Liars, Exiles
Associated Season: Middle Spring
Personality: Cheerful, Hyper, Scatterbrained
Common Pronouns: he/him, they/them
Sacred Land: The Rainbow Rocks
Other Information:
  • His most popular depiction blends elements of foxes and butterflies. He is often depicted with a halo of light and glowing slightly.
  • His title comes from his dreamy nature and flighty pursuits. This makes him a rival to Ebonik, who is characterized as more of a realist.
  • “Pirism” comes from a corruption on “prism”.
