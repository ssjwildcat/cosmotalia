Unikirns

A horse-like creature with draconic features which is used for transport all over Cosmotalia.

Gigant Bees

Giant honeybees, often kept for their honey.

Gigant Moths

Giant moths, often kept for silk making or for sacred practices.

Gigant Eels

Giant eels, sometimes used for transportation or food (is an acquired taste).

Pronghorns

Antelope beasts kept for transport.

Pelagorn

Large sea-faring bird-like beasts with large serrated beaks, used by fishers for aid in retrieving their catch.

Hippogriffs

Chimeric beast with feline, horse and avian traits. Rarely tamed for transport because their territorial nature.

Hippocampi

Chimeric beast with fish and horse traits. Gentle-natured and often used for underwater transportation.

Pseudodrakes

Tiny dragon-like beasts kept as pets or for sending messages. Come in a wide array of species, morphs and colors.
